  • France

    Paris, France is known for its iconic landmarks, world-renowned art and fashion, café culture and romantic charm. From the Eiffel Tower to the cobbled streets of Montmartre, every part of the city tells a story—one filled with history, beauty and creativity.

  • Kyoto, Japan

    Kyoto is famous for its thousand temples and shrines, traditional Japanese culture, and as a center for refined cuisine and geisha districts like Gion. As a former imperial capital, it's home to numerous historic landmarks, such as the Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion) and Fushimi Inari Shrine, along with serene gardens and traditional arts.

  • Bali, Indonesia

    Bali is famous for its stunning natural beauty, including its beaches, rice terraces, and volcanoes, as well as its rich Hindu culture, vibrant art scene, and delicious cuisine. It is a popular tourist destination known for iconic temples, unique crafts, and a wide range of activities like surfing and diving

  • New York, USA

    After the state was formed, its government met in various cities, including New York City and Kingston. The legislature ultimately decided to establish a permanent capital in Albany in 1797.

  • Cape Town, South Africa

    home to iconic mountain ranges, stunning natural wonders, thrilling landscapes, and ocean vistas, Cape Town offers no shortage of adventures. Known as the adventure capital of South Africa, the lifestyle of locals and visitors alike centres around the outdoors and an active lifestyle.
